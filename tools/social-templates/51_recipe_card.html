Creamy Garlic Pasta
A quick & delicious weeknight dinner
⏱ 25 min
👥 Serves 4
📊 Easy
Ingredients
• 400g spaghetti
• 4 cloves garlic, minced
• 200ml heavy cream
• 100g parmesan cheese
• 2 tbsp olive oil
• Salt & pepper to taste
• Fresh basil for garnish
Steps
1. Boil pasta until al dente
2. Sauté garlic in olive oil
3. Add cream, simmer 3 min
4. Stir in parmesan cheese
5. Toss pasta in sauce
6. Season & garnish with basil
7. Serve immediately!
