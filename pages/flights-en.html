How to Find Cheap Flights

Tue/Wed Cheapest

Airlines通常在週二、三推出促銷，機票價格比週五六日平20-30%。

Book 1-3 Months Early

Short-haul: book 4-6 weeks ahead. Long-haul: 2-3 months. This usually gets the best prices.

Use Price Comparison

Trip.com offers price trends and prediction tools. Set alerts to get notified when prices drop.

Red-eye Flights Cheaper

Early morning flights are usually cheaper. Arrive and start your trip right away.

Connecting Flights Save More

Direct flights are convenient but pricier. One connection can save up to 40%. Allow at least 2hr layover.

Budget vs Full-Service

HK Express, Peach offer lower fares but charge for baggage. Compare total cost including luggage.

Flight FAQs

Q: What's the cheapest flight from Hong Kong to Tokyo?
香港飛東京來回Economy incl. tax約HK$1,280起。HK Express廉航促銷時可低至HK$980。建議提早4-6星期預訂，避開日本黃金週及暑假旺季。週二三出發通常最平。
Q: HK Express同Cathay Pacific邊間抵？
HK Express has lower base fares but excludes baggage and meals. Adding 20kg checked bag (HK$200-300) may close the gap with Cathay. Choose HK Express for 1-2 day trips, Cathay for longer trips with more luggage.
Q: When is the cheapest time to buy flights?
短途（日韓泰）：出發前4-6星期。長途（歐美）：出發前2-3個月。每年1月、5月及9月是Airlines大促銷季節。週二三下午經常有限時特價。
Q: How long is the flight from Hong Kong to Bangkok?
香港直飛曼谷約2.5hr，是香港人最受歡迎的短途Destination之一。來回含稅約HK$880起。Thai Lion Air、HK Express經常有促銷低至HK$600。
Q: Can I cancel flights for free?
Depends on fare type. Full-service flexible fares usually allow free changes/cancellation. Budget basic fares are non-refundable — add "flexible rebooking" option (~HK$100-200). Some Trip.com fares offer "Free Cancellation".
Q: 香港飛首爾最平Airlines？
HK Express及Air Busan通常提供最平價格，來回約HK$1,180起。國泰及Korean Air含行李餐飲約HK$2,000起。促銷期間HK Express可低至HK$800。
Q: How much are budget airline baggage fees?
HK Express checked 20kg: HK$200-350 (varies by route), carry-on 7kg free. Peach baggage: HK$180-280. Add baggage at booking — airport counter is 50%+ more expensive.

Hong Kong Flight Route Guide

Japan — Hong Kong's Favourite Destination

Japan is the #1 destination for Hong Kong travellers. With the weak yen, travel offers exceptional value. Tokyo return from HK$1,280, Osaka HK$1,180, Okinawa HK$980. Cathay Pacific and HK Express are the most popular airlines. Both Narita and Haneda airports have direct flights, ~4 hours. Avoid Golden Week (late Apr-early May) and Obon (mid-Aug) when prices jump 50%+. Cherry blossom season (late Mar-mid Apr) and autumn foliage (Nov) are best but book 2 months ahead.

South Korea — K-pop & Food Paradise

Seoul return from HK$1,380, the second most popular destination. Incheon Airport has convenient airport express to Seoul Station. HK Express, Korean Air and Asiana all fly direct. Busan is rising in popularity, return from HK$1,180 — Haeundae Beach and Gamcheon Culture Village are Instagram hotspots. Korea offers 90-day visa-free entry. Avoid Chuseok and Lunar New Year when prices surge.

Southeast Asia — Best Budget Short-Haul

Bangkok return from HK$880 is one of the cheapest short-haul destinations. Singapore HK$1,080, Vietnam HK$980, Bali HK$1,680. Rich airline choice with both full-service and budget carriers. Thailand offers 30-day visa-free. Vietnam requires e-visa. Avoid rainy season (Jun-Oct); Nov-Feb has the most comfortable weather.

Taiwan — Shortest & Most Convenient

Taipei return from HK$780 with just a 1.5-hour flight — one of the shortest international routes. Cathay, China Airlines, EVA Air, HK Express all have multiple daily flights. Taoyuan Airport Express goes direct to Taipei Main Station. Night markets, Jiufen, Taipei 101 are must-visits. 30-day visa-free entry.

Europe & Americas — Book Early, Save Big

London return from HK$4,280, Paris HK$4,580, New York HK$5,880. Long-haul prices fluctuate significantly — booking 2-3 months early can save up to 30%. Cathay Pacific offers direct HK-London, New York, Paris flights (12-16 hours). Connecting flights via Dubai or Singapore are usually cheaper but take longer. Book fares including baggage for long trips.

Route-Specific Price FAQs

Q: What are the cheapest Hong Kong to Osaka flights?
Hong Kong to Osaka (Kansai Airport) return from HK$1,180 incl. tax. HK Express and Peach Aviation are cheapest, sales as low as HK$880. Cathay Pacific with baggage/meals from HK$1,800. ~3.5hr flight, book 4-6 weeks early.
Q: Are there direct flights from Hong Kong to Okinawa?
Yes! HK Express and Peach Aviation offer direct flights to Okinawa (Naha Airport), ~2.5 hours. Return from HK$980 incl. tax. Okinawa is Japan's closest resort — perfect for 3-4 day beach trips.
Q: Best airline from Hong Kong to Singapore?
Singapore Airlines has the best service but costs more (from HK$1,800). Cathay Pacific offers good value (from HK$1,400). Scoot is the budget option (from HK$1,080 excl. baggage). ~3.5hr flight.
Q: When are Hong Kong to Taipei flights cheapest?
HK to Taipei is the shortest international route (1.5hr), return from HK$780. Weekdays (Tue-Thu) are 30% cheaper. Avoid CNY, Dragon Boat, Mid-Autumn. Late Apr-May and Oct-Nov are best travel seasons.
Q: Cathay Pacific vs HK Express to Japan — which is better?
Cathay includes 23kg baggage + meals + seat selection, Tokyo return from HK$2,200. HK Express base fare HK$1,280 plus baggage (HK$200-350). With 20kg luggage, the gap is only HK$300-500 — Cathay offers much better value. Light 2-3 day trips: HK Express. Normal trips: Cathay.
Q: Cheapest Hong Kong to London flights? How long?
Cathay Pacific direct to Heathrow ~12hr, return from HK$4,280. Via Dubai (Emirates) from HK$3,500 but 18-20hr total. British Airways direct from HK$4,500. Book 2-3 months early. Summer and Christmas are peak pricing.
